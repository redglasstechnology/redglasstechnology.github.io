ABOUT


We build real-time digital ad spaces on the tabletops of cafes, restaurants and food courts. Why? These are one of the most visited places in the world because everyone needs to eat! The world is becoming digital but people still spend a significant amount of time offline, especially during a meal time. A typical cafe in the KL city center has over 1000 visitors in a week. In fact, many people are treating cafe and some restaurants as a place between office and home. Therefore, we place our digital platforms on the tabletops of these places to provide the visitors with information that might be of interest to them. The information that we provide is not limited to interactive advertisements, but we also show fun facts, health facts, events happening in town and the headline of the newspaper today.

WHY CHOOSE US?


Displaying your products and services to the right people at the right time on a physical digital space allows new people to discover you. Online advertisements are so highly targeted to the extent that it becomes a challenge for non-relevant people to know about you but they can be your potential customers as well! By using a physical platform that is connected to the internet, we optimize between the effectiveness and the exposure of your contents. This allows both targeted and new audience to discover you!
