ABOUT


We build the digital display software that creates an engaging and interactive experience at food and beverage outlets, and events. Why? Customers do not feel a sense of belonging and loyalty at a physical space. We believe that a physical space should store sweet memories of the past created by your customers. That's why we are here to create an experience where your customers feel belonged and coming back!

OUR PRODUCT

A 42-inch wall-mounted smart digital display is installed at food and beverage outlets or at events to improve customer engagement and create great customer experiences at their physical space.

Our technology captures and displays live social media content shared by the customers onto the smart digital screen. In this way, it creates an interactive and memorable atmosphere at the store or at an event, which can keep your customers coming back.

We also provide owners and event organizers a Redglass content management platform where they can manage the content of the smart digital screen.
